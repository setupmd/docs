---

Our goal is to highlight some must have plugins for Minecraft, in order for them to make the list they have to meet the below criteria.

<ul class="pl-5 list-disc">
    <li>Publish to reputable marketplaces</li>
    <li>Premium plugins should be on Spigot marketplace</li>
    <li>Provide a benefit to either server owners or it's players</li>
    <li>Reputable development team</li>
</ul>

<small>This is not a definitive list and may change as we add more plugins</small>
